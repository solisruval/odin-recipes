How to Make A Quesadilla

This recipe will help you become the MVP at your next Cinco De Mayo party

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Flour Tortilla
  • Corn
  • Green bell peppers
  • black beans

Steps:

  1. wash the black bean and set aside
  2. deseed and dice the green bell pepper
  3. take the corn off the cobb
  4. heat up the comal at medium heat. this will prevent the tortilla from heating up too quickly and burning/ hardening
  5. add tortilla to heat until warm to the touch
  6. add cheese
  7. once cheese starts melting, add all other ingredients

Enjoy!! Drink with mexican beer and margaritas!
